Our networked version of Joseph Addison and Richard Steele's periodical The Spectator (1711-12) provides a new way to trace different themes and discourses through the publication's full run. The Spectator is famous for contributing to formation of the bourgeois public sphere in early eighteenth-century England. Here you can explore topics that came to characterize this space.
